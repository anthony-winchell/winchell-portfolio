Career Goals

My vision of a successful career in the tech industry is one where I can work collaboratively with a team to solve complex problems in an ethical and secure manner to ensure the confidentiality of the information of our customers. I would like to work in a field where there is always something new to learn or a new problem to solve while also having the support of a team of like-minded individuals who I can learn from.

Upon graduating from Western Governors University, I plan to have at least one internship and an offer to become a full-time employee. With this in mind, I must use the opportunity of a college education to ensure that by the time I am ready to enter the workforce, I already have the skills necessary to succeed. College is an exciting opportunity to learn new skills I can use in a career.

In the future, I see myself as a senior developer who others can feel comfortable coming to for help. I enjoy working with others to solve a problem. I want to set an environment where it is okay for people to ask others for help or work collaboratively to come up with a solution. The development process should be social. Our experiences differ, and we can use those different experiences to help each other.
